## Section 2.1 — Expanding the Argument

The previous chapter introduced the main theme. Now is the time to deepen
it. Provide evidence, examples, or narrative that gives the theme texture
and weight.

Lists are an effective way to enumerate points clearly:

- First supporting idea
- Second supporting idea
- Third supporting idea

Numbered lists work when order or sequence matters:

1. Start with the most important point.
2. Follow with supporting detail.
3. Conclude with an implication or transition.

## Section 2.2 — Handling Complexity
